About This Division

Bermuda's source for quality aggregates

Island Quarry Ltd. (IQL) officially opened on 25 October 1991 and is located at 9 Coney Island Road, Hamilton Parish. It is the only hard-rock quarry in Bermuda producing aggregates for residential, commercial, and government projects.

Whether you need sand for a driveway, 5/8 rock for drainage, or bulk gabion rock for coastal works, IQL coordinates delivery or pickup to suit your project requirements.

Aggregate Products

Sand

Screenings

5/8 Rock
