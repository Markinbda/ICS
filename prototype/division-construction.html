About This Division

Heavy-industry capability built over six decades

Island Construction Services began specialising in excavation, demolition, and landscaping. Over the years ICS became the island's leader in trenching, crane services, container haulage, trucking, asbestos abatement, mould removal, project management, and aggregate supply.

Almost entirely Bermudian staffed, ICS supports residential, commercial, and government clients who need dependable heavy-services coordination from a single experienced local operator.
